# Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how My Leadership Quest ("MLQ", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information we collect
- Account info: name, email, age (derived from date of birth), optional school and parent email.
- App activity: goals, daily goals, streaks, coins, and interactions needed to power MLQ features.
- Device info: basic device data for security and performance (e.g., OS, app version).
- Notifications & messaging preferences.

## 2. How we use data
- To create and manage your account.
- To power app features: goal tracking, AI coaching, reminders, and rewards.
- To improve safety, performance, and user experience.
- To send important notices (e.g., password resets, updates).

## 3. AI processing
- MLQ may use AI to personalize coaching. We minimize personal data sent to processing services and anonymize when possible.
- Sensitive personal data is not used for marketing.

## 4. Children’s privacy
- MLQ is designed for young users with parental guidance where required.
- Parent email (if provided) is used for account support and safety notifications.

## 5. Data sharing
- We do not sell your personal data.
- We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., authentication, payments, analytics) strictly to provide the service and as required by law.

## 6. Data retention
- We retain data while your account is active or as needed for legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, legal compliance).
- You may request deletion via support.

## 7. Security
- We use industry-standard measures to protect your data.
- No online system is 100% secure; please keep your credentials safe.

## 8. Your choices
- You can update profile information in-app.
- You can opt into/out of certain notifications.
- You may request data export or deletion by contacting support.

## 9. International transfers
- Data may be processed in regions where our service providers operate, with safeguards in place.

## 10. Changes to this Policy
- We may update this Policy. We’ll post the latest version in the app. Continued use means you accept the changes.

## 11. Contact
For privacy requests, contact: privacy@myleadershipquest.app
