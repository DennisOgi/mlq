# Refund Policy

Last updated: August 2025

We want you to love My Leadership Quest ("MLQ"). If you experience issues, please read this Refund Policy.

## 1. Free trial
- If a free trial is available, you will not be charged until the trial ends. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges.

## 2. Subscription refunds
- We generally do not provide refunds for partially used billing periods.
- If you believe you were charged in error or had a technical issue preventing use, please contact us within 14 days of the transaction and we will review your case.

## 3. Duplicate or unauthorized charges
- If you find a duplicate charge or suspect unauthorized activity, contact us immediately. We will investigate and assist with resolving the issue.

## 4. How to request a refund
- Email support with your account email, purchase date, and transaction reference.
- We may request additional information to verify the purchase.

## 5. Payment processing
- Payments are handled by our payment partner. Some refunds may be processed according to their policies and timelines.

## 6. Changes to this Policy
- We may update this Refund Policy. The latest version will be available in the app.

## 7. Contact
Email: billing@myleadershipquest.app
